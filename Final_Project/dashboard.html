Simple Dashboard for health system monitoring

The web-based dashboard is designed to give regional health leaders the tools they need to monitor an non-communicable disease program. Surveillance teams can conduct day-to-day management of activities and higher level monthly and quarterly reports give managers a high level view of progress.



  1. Daily, monthly, and quarterly reports

  2. Hypertension Control

  3. Registrations

  4. Follow-up rates

  5. Overdue patient lists

  6. Treatment inertia

  7. Secure

  8. Role-based access levels
Regional Reports

View monthly and quarterly reports of hyprtension control metrics across a district or state. Compare the facilities to see where your hypertension program is working and where it can be strengthened.

Facility reports

Monitor individual facilities and benchmark facilities against each other. Facility reports give each hospital's team a view into their success in managing their patients with hypertension.

Downoad patient line lists

You own your own data. Download your data to run custom reports or work in Excel.

Monitor overdue patients

The dashboard gives you tools to generate lists of patients who have missed follow-up visits. Send community health workers to conduct in-person follow-ups, create call lists, or manage a call center to follow-up with patients.

Manage your program

The dashboard also includes administration features for adding new facilities, approving users, managing common medications for the Simple app, etc.
